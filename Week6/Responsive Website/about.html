About

If you look on the back of some of your favorite old records, there's a good chance you'll see the Music Studio Services name. Music Studio Services opened back in the late sixties and was the launch pad for many legendary artists such as David Bowie, Elton John, Queen and Marc Bolan. The names don't stop there. Music Studio Services was also booked by The Beatles (and later on Ringo Starr, George Harrison, John Lennon and Paul McCartney as solo artists / producers), Lou Reed, Carly Simon, Genesis, Supertramp, Black Sabbath.
